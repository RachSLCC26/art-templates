About

Hi, I'm Rachel Nelson

Welcome to my little corner of the internet! I’m a stay-at-home mama, and I’m passionate about art, design, organization, creativity, etc. This website is a reflection of my love for design and my journey to create something that brings value to others.

I started this project because I believe in the power of creativity, organization, and helping others. I wanted to share my ideas, my work, and the resources I’ve created to help others like you. Whether you’re here to find inspiration, educational content, etc. or just to learn more about me, I’m so glad you stopped by!

A little about me: I’m passionate about helping people embrace their creativity, especially in the chaos of everyday life. I believe that even in our busiest moments, we can find time for the things that bring us joy—whether it’s making something beautiful or simply organizing our day. This website is my way of sharing that mission. When I’m not working, you can usually find me crocheting, riding my bike, or daydreaming about my next big adventure with my family. I believe creativity is a lifelong journey, and I’m always looking for new ways to express myself and inspire others along the way.

"I believe that art is a form of self-expression that is accessible to everyone. It’s not just about creating something beautiful—it’s about capturing emotions, telling stories, and finding joy in the process. Whether it’s through design, drawing, or simply organizing your space, art has the power to inspire, connect, and bring peace to our busy lives.

Thank you for visiting, and I hope you find something here that sparks your creativity or helps you on your own journey!
