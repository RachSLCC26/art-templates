Hi, I'm Rachel

I’m a mama, maker, and creative soul who believes that art has the power to bring people together — especially at home with our littles. I started this space to share meaningful art ideas, printable templates, and fun creative moments you can do side-by-side with your kids.

This isn’t about being perfect. It’s about showing up with love, some paint-stained fingers, and the desire to connect through creativity — whether it’s five minutes before bedtime or a rainy afternoon with nothing planned.

You’ll find a mix of downloadable templates, inspiration for simple art projects, and encouragement for embracing the mess and magic of creativity at home.

When I’m not creating, I’m probably chasing toddlers, crocheting something cuddly, or dreaming up our next big family adventure.

Thanks for stopping by — I hope you find something here that sparks joy, connection, and maybe even a new tradition with your little ones.
